What is the slope?

The slope is represented by the coefficient values returned by model.coef_. Each coefficient shows how much the marks change when a feature increases by one unit while keeping other features constant.

What is the intercept?

The intercept is the starting value of the prediction when all feature values are zero.

What do they mean?

The coefficients indicate feature importance and direction of impact. Positive coefficients mean marks increase as the feature increases. The intercept acts as the baseline prediction.

What is MAE?

Mean Absolute Error measures the average absolute difference between actual and predicted values. Lower MAE indicates better model performance.

What is R² Score?

R² Score measures how well the model explains the variation in the target variable. Values closer to 1 indicate a better fit.

Is the model performing well?

Yes. If MAE is low and R² is close to 1, the model is performing well.
